Dessert Recipes

Chocolate Mousse

Ingredients

  • Dark chocolate
  • Egg whites
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Melt chocolate.
  2. Whip egg whites with sugar.
  3. Fold together and chill.

Fruit Salad

Ingredients

  • Seasonal fruits
  • Honey
  • Lemon

Steps

  1. Chop fruits.
  2. Toss with honey and lemon.
  3. Serve chilled.
